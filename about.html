About Us

Our Story

Oxide Consulting was founded out of a passion to empower small businesses with the online presence they deserve. After seeing numerous businesses overpay for poorly designed, prebuilt websites, as a seasoned professional developer, I knew there was a better way. With a dedication to providing real online representation, we aim to give small organizations the edge they need to thrive in a competitive digital market.

Let's face it: everything has gotten pretty expensive, and services are no exception. As prices skyrocketed and businesses had a harder time making money, we saw an opportunity to help. With the majority of "Marketing" companies, many businesses end up with outdated, poorly designed websites that do little to help their growth- and they overpay for them. Other times, you'll see an attractive price on the front page of their website, but when the work is complete, the saying echoes: "You get what you pay for." But not at Oxide. From the first conversation, our goal is to provide value- not a cheap, disappointing service.

This wasn't just an opportunity for us, but a real issue for small businesses trying to get on even footing with large corporations. Out of this came our game-changing $0 down, $149 per month Web Presence Package. Our Web Presence Package is the best way for small businesses to obtain a high-quality, customized, and relevant online presence that will keep them competitive for years to come. And that's our mission. Steve Jobs once said "Focus is about saying no" and we agree. No gimmicks, no shortcuts, no hassle.

-Adam M, Owner
